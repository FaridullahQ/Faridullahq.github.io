Wireshark is one of the best tools for developing packet-level understanding.

## What Wireshark helps with

It allows you to capture and inspect network traffic in detail. That makes it useful for:

- seeing how systems communicate
- filtering for specific protocols or hosts
- reviewing requests and responses
- spotting unusual patterns or behaviors

## Beginner learning goals

When starting with Wireshark, the first priorities are:

- understanding the interface
- learning how to capture traffic
- applying display filters
- identifying common protocols
- reading packet details with patience

## Why this tool matters in cybersecurity

Packet visibility teaches how communication really works. It is easier to understand suspicious traffic, connection attempts, and protocol behavior when you can see the packets directly.

Wireshark is not just a tool. It is a way to train your eyes for network investigation.
